# [Workstream Name] — Decisions Log

**Append-only.** Never edit or delete past entries. If a decision is reversed,
add a new entry citing the prior one.

Each entry captures *what* was decided, *when*, and *why* — so future-you (or
a stranger reading the workstream cold) can understand the reasoning without
re-litigating it.

## Format

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — Short decision title

**Decision:** What was decided, in one sentence.

**Why:** The reasoning. What problem this solved, what alternative was
rejected, what trade-off was accepted.

**Alternatives considered:** (optional) Other paths that were on the table
and why they lost.

**Consequences:** (optional) What this commits us to going forward — what
becomes easy, what becomes hard.
```
